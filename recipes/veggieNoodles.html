Veggie Noodles Recipe

Description

Veggie Nooles are a happy and heathly lunch or dinner idea.

They are easy to make and even easier to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Cooked Noodles
  • Carrot
  • Sweet Pepper
  • Broccoli
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Chop veggies into bit size pieces and chop garlic very small
  2. Pour small amount of olive oil into wok and turn on heat to medium-high
  3. Carrot and Sweet Pepper for 3 mins
  4. Add Broccoli and stir fry for 3-5 mins until everything is almost tender
  5. Add tomato and stir fry for 1 min
  6. Add in garlic and stir fry for 30 seconds
  7. Add in Cooked Noodles and Soy souce. Stir fry for 15 seconds
  8. Serve in bowl or plate and enojoy
