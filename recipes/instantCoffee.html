Instant Coffee Recipe

Description

Instant Coffee is a great way to get a quick boost.

It is easy to make and gets you ready to do your work.

Ingredients

  • Electric Kettle with 2 cups of water in it
  • Instant Coffee Mix
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Turn on kettle and wait until water boils and it turns itself off
  2. Take a teaspon of the instant coffee mix and put it into a mug
  3. Pour hot water into the mug and stir the instant coffee. Making sure all the powder is disolved.
  4. If desired add a little milk and or sugar then stir until well blended.
  5. Wait until the coffee cools slighly then drink and enjoy
