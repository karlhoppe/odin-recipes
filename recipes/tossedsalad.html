Tossed Salad Recipe

Description

Tossed salad is a happy and heathly lunch or dinner idea.

It is easy to make and even easier to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Carrot
  • Sweet Pepper
  • Red Onion
  • Itailian Seasoning Mix
  • Vinegar
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash all veggies
  2. Chop all veggies into bit size pieces
  3. Place all chopped veggies into bowl which is slightly too large
  4. Sprinkle some Itailian Seasoning, vinegar,and oil on the top of the salad
  5. Break out the salad tongs and go crazy tossing the salad as high as you can go.
  6. Either eat straight from the bowl or serve in smaller bowl(s). Enjoy!
